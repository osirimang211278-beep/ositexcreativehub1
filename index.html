Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before working with us

Project timelines vary depending on the scope of work. A logo design typically takes 3 to 5 business days, a full brand identity takes 1 to 2 weeks, a website takes 2 to 4 weeks, and a digital marketing campaign setup takes not less than 3 hours. We always discuss timelines clearly before starting any project.

Our pricing depends on the specific service and scope of your project. We offer flexible packages to suit different budgets. Click the Get Started button and tell us about your project — we will send you a detailed quote within 24 hours.

Yes absolutely! Ositex Creative Hub works with clients worldwide. We communicate via email, WhatsApp, and video calls to ensure smooth collaboration regardless of your location.

Getting started is simple. Click the Get Started button anywhere on this website, fill in your project details, and our team will get back to you within 24 hours with a plan and quote tailored specifically to your needs.

We offer up to 3 free revisions on all design projects to ensure you are completely satisfied with the final result. Additional revisions beyond that are available at a small fee. Our goal is always to deliver work you are proud of.

The more details you share the better. For design projects we need your brand colors, preferred style, and examples of designs you like. For web development we need your content and any specific features you want. For marketing we need your target audience and business goals. Do not worry if you are not sure — we will guide you through the process.

We accept bank transfers and mobile money payments. We typically require a 50% deposit before starting a project and the remaining 50% upon completion. Full payment details will be shared when we send your project quote.

Yes we do. We provide a simple project agreement that outlines the scope of work, timeline, payment terms, and revision policy. This protects both you and us and ensures everyone is on the same page from the start.

Still have questions? We are happy to help!
